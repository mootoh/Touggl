Touggl

Touch NFC tag to start/stop the tasks on Toggl.com.

Android app on Google Play

An experiment to utilize NFC in a real life. Touch a NFC tag (ex. Clipper/Pasmo/Suica card) with your Android phone, to start/stop the task on http://www.toggl.com.

Requirement

  • Android phone with NFC and ICS, like Galaxy Nexus
  • Toggl account

Restriction

It does not sync with your online activity on Toggl web correctly. Just use only Touggl and forget about recording at Toggl web site, stick to it.
